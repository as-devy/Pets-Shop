Terms and Conditions

1. Pet Agreement

By submitting a request to rehome or foster a pet, you agree to provide a safe and loving home for the pet.

2. Responsibilities

You must provide adequate food, water, shelter, and medical care. Neglect or abuse of the pet will result in legal action.

3. Home Environment

Pets should not be left alone for extended periods. A secure and pet-friendly environment is required.

4. Financial Commitment

Pet owners must be financially prepared for food, veterinary care, and other related expenses.

5. Return Policy

If you can no longer care for the pet, you must contact the original rehoming service for guidance instead of abandoning the pet.

6. Agreement Acceptance

By rehoming or fostering a pet, you confirm that you have read and understood these terms and agree to comply with them.
